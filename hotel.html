Mountian view Hotel

The Fifth Season Hut is a resort located 2360 meters Above the Sea Level in the picturesque village of Juta in the Kazbegi region. It is a wonderful place to enjoy mountains with a superb view of the Chaukhi array. It is equipped with both in-door and out-door cafes and comfortable rooms. Here you can always enjoy good music, find perfect places for camping or horse riding and many other options for those who like active recreation.
